Hobbies

Piano

This is a recent hobby I have picked up, I am still a beginner. I use the keys to produce music, but I've always loved the sound of the piano and I've been recently inspired to learn how to properly play. I hope to one day be good enough to play in front of an audience.

Music Production

The most creative thing I do besides designing websites. I get a lot of joy from creating music. I mainly produce house music, but also like to dabble in other sub genres of EDM and pop music. I hope to one day produce professional quality music and to eventually release my own album.

DJ

I love to DJ more than any other music related thing I do. I first started in middle school and never looked back. I will sometimes accept paid gigs on the weekends for extra spending money, but the main goal is to travel and play at all of the world's biggest music festivals.
